\begin{defn*}{Interior Point}
  Fix $S \subseteq \mathbb{R} ^{n}$, we say that $x \in \mathbb{R} ^{n}$ is an interior point of $S$ if there exists $ \varepsilon > 0$  such that $ B\left(x, \varepsilon\right) \subseteq S$ 
\end{defn*}
