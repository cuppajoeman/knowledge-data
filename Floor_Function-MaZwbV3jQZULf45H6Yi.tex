\begin{defn*}{Floor Function}
Of the form $\left\lfloor \cdot \right\rfloor: \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{Z}$, it returns the largest integer less than or equal to it. That is:

\[
\left\lfloor x \right\rfloor =  x  -  \varepsilon
\]  

where $ \varepsilon \in [0, 1)$ and $ x  -  \varepsilon \in \mathbb{Z}$ 
\end{defn*}
