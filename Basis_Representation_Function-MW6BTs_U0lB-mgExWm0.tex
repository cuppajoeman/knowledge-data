\begin{defn*}{Basis Representation Function}
Let $\mathcal{B} = \left( b_{1}, b_{2},  \dots ,b_{n - 1},b_{n} \right)$ be a basis for a finite dimensional vector space $V$. Then for $\vec{x} \in V$, $\exists ! c_{1}, c_{2},  \dots ,c_{n - 1},c_{n} \in \mathbb{R}$ such that
\[
 \vec{x}= \sum_{i=1}^{n} c_{i}b_{i}
\]

Then we define the function $\left[ \vec{ \text{ } } \right]_{\mathcal{B}} : V \to \mathbb{R}^{n}$: 
\[
\left[ \vec{x} \right]_{\mathcal{B}}  {\stackrel{\text{def}}{=}} \begin{bmatrix} c_1 \\ c_2 \\ \vdots \\ c_n \end{bmatrix}
\]


\end{defn*}
