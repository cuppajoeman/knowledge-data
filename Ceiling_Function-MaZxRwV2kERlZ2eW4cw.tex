\begin{defn*}{Ceiling Function}
Of the form $\left\lfloor \cdot \right\rfloor: \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{Z}$, it returns the smallest integer greater than or equal to it. That is:

\[
\left\lceil x \right\rceil =  x  +  \varepsilon
\]  

where $ \varepsilon \in [0, 1)$ and $ x  +  \varepsilon \in \mathbb{Z}$ 
\end{defn*}
