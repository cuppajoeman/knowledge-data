\begin{defn*}{Set Union}
The union of two sets $A$ and $B$ is the set of elements which are in $A$, in $B$, or in both $A$ and $B$. Symbolically:
\[
	A \cup B =\left\{ x: x \in A \text{ or } x \in B \right\}
\]
\end{defn*}
