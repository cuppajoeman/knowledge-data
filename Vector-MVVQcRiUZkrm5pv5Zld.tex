\begin{defn*}{Vector}
Let $x \in  \mathbb{F} ^{n} $, when we think of $x$ as the arrow starting at $0 \in  \mathbb{F} ^{n} $  and ending at $x$, then refer to $x$  as a vector and write it as $ \vec{x}$ 
\end{defn*}
