\begin{defn*}{Cartesian Product}
Given two sets $A$, $B$ the Cartesian product of $A$ and $B$ written as $A\times B$ is the set
\[
	\left\{ \left( a, b \right): a \in A, b \in B \right\}
\]
\end{defn*}
