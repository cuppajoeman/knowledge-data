\begin{defn*}{Addition in $\mathbb{F}^n$}
Addition in $\mathbb{F}^{n}$ is defined by adding corresponding coordinates:
\[
	 \left(x_{1}, \ldots, x_{n}\right)+\left(y_{1}, \ldots, y_{n}\right)=\left(x_{1}+y_{1}, \ldots, x_{n}+y_{n}\right) 
\]
\end{defn*}
